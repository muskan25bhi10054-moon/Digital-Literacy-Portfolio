# Part B: "Stay Safe Online" Prevention Checklist #

# To remain secure as a Digital Ambassador, follow these 8 actionable tips:
1) UPI PIN Security: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required to send money.
2) Use 2FA: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all email, banking, and social media accounts.
3) Verify QR Codes: Before scanning a QR code for payment, verify the name of the recipient displayed in the app.
4) Avoid Public Wi-Fi: Never conduct financial transactions or log into sensitive accounts while using public or unsecured Wi-Fi.
5) Check URLs: Always look for the "https://" and the padlock icon before entering details on a website.
6) Strong Passwords: Use a unique, complex password for every account and change your banking passwords every 90 days.
7) Financial Literacy: Regularly check your bank statements for small, unauthorized transactions that may go unnoticed.
8) Reporting Channels: If you are a victim of cybercrime, immediately report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the national Helpline 1930.
